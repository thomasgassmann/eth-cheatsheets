\subsection*{PSD}
$M \in \mathbb{R}^{n\times n}$ PSD $\Leftrightarrow \forall x \in \mathbb{R}^n: x^TMx \geq 0 \\
\Leftrightarrow$ all principal minors of $M$ have non-negative determinant\\
$\Leftrightarrow \lambda \geq 0 \ \forall \lambda\in\sigma(M)$
